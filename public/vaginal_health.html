Prevention & Care Tips

Proactive measures for maintaining optimal vaginal health

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water to maintain natural moisture and help flush toxins from your system.

Probiotic-Rich Diet

Include yogurt, kefir, and fermented foods to support healthy vaginal microbiome balance.

Breathable Clothing

Wear cotton underwear and avoid tight clothing to promote air circulation and moisture control.

Gentle Cleansing

Use mild, unscented soap and water externally only - never douche or use harsh products.

Safe Practices

Always use protection during sexual activity and practice good hygiene before and after intimacy.

Regular Check-ups

Schedule annual gynecological exams and pap tests as recommended by your healthcare provider.
